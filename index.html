Satoru Gojo

About Gojo

Satoru Gojo, is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer. He is one of the main protagonists of Jujutsu Kaisen.

Gojo and his Students

Gojo's Characteristics

  1. He has light blue eyes
  2. He is known to be the strongest Jujutsu Sorcerer
  3. He is 6 foot 3 tall

Gojo's Students

Gojo teaches 3 students. They are jujutsu sorcerer as well.

  • Yuji Itadori
  • Nobara Kugisaki
  • Megumi Fushiguro
